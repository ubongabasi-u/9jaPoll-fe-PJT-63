PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATES

PETER OBI

PDP

Peter Gregory Obi (born on the 19 of July 1961) is a Nigerian politician and businessman who was the vice presidential candidate in the 2019 Nigerian general election under the People's Democratic Party. He was the former governor of Anambra State.

YAHAYA BELLO

LP

Yahaya Adoza Bello (born June 18, 1975) is a Nigerian politician, businessman, chartered fellow of the Association of National Accountants of Nigeria and the current Governor of Kogi State.

YEMI OSINBAJO

APC

Oluyemi Oluleke Osinbajo GCON (born 8 March 1957), is a lawyer and politician who is currently serving as the Vice President of Nigeria, since 29th May, 2015.

ROCHAS OKOROCHA

APGA

Owelle Rochas Anayo Okorocha (born 22 September 1962) is a Nigerian philanthropist and politician of Igbo extraction from Imo State. He is the Senator representing Imo West Senatorial district at the 9th Nigerian Senate.

DONALD DUKE

SDP

Donald Duke (born 30 September 1961 in Calabar) is a Nigerian politician. He was the Governor of Cross River State, Nigeria from 29 May 1999 to 29 May 2007 and the 2019 Nigeria Presidential candidate for Social Democratic Party, SDP.
